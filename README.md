# Hr-analytics-employee-attrition-analysis
End-to-end HR Analytics project using Python, SQL, and Power BI to analyze employee attrition, identify key retention factors, and deliver actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and data-driven recommendation
## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition data to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover. Using Python, SQL, and Power BI, the project provides actionable insights to help organizations improve employee retention and workforce management.

## Problem Statement

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations, leading to increased recruitment costs and reduced productivity. The objective of this project is to identify the drivers of employee attrition and provide data-driven recommendations to improve employee retention.

## Key Insights

* Employees working overtime have higher attrition rates.
* Low job satisfaction is strongly associated with employee turnover.
* Sales department shows the highest attrition.
* Employees with lower income levels are more likely to leave.
* Most attrition occurs within the first few years of employment.

## Business Recommendations

* Improve employee engagement programs.
* Reduce excessive overtime.
* Enhance career growth opportunities.
* Review compensation and benefits structure.
* Conduct regular employee satisfaction surveys.
